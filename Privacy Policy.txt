Recordum Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Introduction

Recordum is a personal journaling application that helps users capture their daily experiences and gain insights using artificial intelligence. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the Recordum application.

Information We Collect

Recordum may collect the following information when you use the app:

Journal entries and notes you create

Information you choose to record about people, places, or activities

Spending records and personal logs entered by the user

Optional voice recordings used for transcription

Optional location information when users attach a location to an entry

Account information such as email address when signing in

Recordum only collects information necessary to provide the app’s functionality.

How We Use Information

Information collected is used to:

Store and organize your personal journal entries

Generate summaries and insights using artificial intelligence

Enable memory search and assistant features

Provide analytics and personal insights about your recorded information

Manage subscriptions and premium features

Artificial Intelligence Processing

Some features of Recordum use artificial intelligence services to analyze journal entries and generate summaries or answer questions about stored information.

Text data required for these features may be securely processed by trusted third-party providers.

Third-Party Services

Recordum may use trusted third-party services to provide core functionality, including:

Supabase for secure authentication and data storage

OpenAI for AI analysis and summaries

RevenueCat for subscription and billing management

Google Play for payment processing

These providers process data only as required to provide the requested features.

Data Storage

User data may be stored securely in cloud infrastructure. Reasonable technical measures are taken to protect data against unauthorized access.

User Control

Users retain control over their information. Users may:

Edit or delete journal entries

Remove stored data

Delete their account where supported

Children’s Privacy

Recordum is not intended for children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Security

Recordum implements reasonable security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
